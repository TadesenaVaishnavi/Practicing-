The Hare and the Tortoise:
A hare was proud of his speed and teased a tortoise for being slow. The tortoise challenged the hare to a race, and the hare accepted. The hare ran quickly and got ahead of the tortoise, but then stopped to take a nap. The tortoise kept walking slowly and eventually won the race. The moral of the story is "slow and steady wins the race".
